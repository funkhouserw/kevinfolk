Hello my name is
KEVIN FOLK

I am a multifaceted designer with a passion for visual metaphor and
a drive to create results driven, visually interesting and conceptually
engaging work in a number of media. I have experience guiding
clients, working under strict deadlines and collaborating with fellow
designers in both an office and personal environment.

I have a deep and unrelenting fascination with fractals, mathematics
and technology. In my spare time I have built my own computer,
played in a Jazz band and traveled across the United States and the
World. I have painted in Florence, drank a Pint in the Guinness
factory in Dublin and wandered the streets of Istanbul. To learn
more about who I am and what I do, please explore my site.
